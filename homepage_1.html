About Us

Who are we ?

Campus Placement is an Placement cell Software which offers organizations with access to fresh talent. They can choose from a large pool of talent and hire in bulk for different roles and verticals. Campus Placement allows organizations to nurture new talent, from the beginning of their career journey, resulting in the development of better performing individuals and a lower attrition rate. Virtual campus recruitment ensures cost and time efficiency, accelerated and effective processes and decreased time-to-hire.
